# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Cacik Yapma" app handles user information.

## Information Collection
The Cacik Yapma app does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app only stores user progress such as streak days locally on the user's device.

## Data Usage
All progress data is stored only on the user's device and is not transmitted to any servers.

## Third-Party Services
This application may use services such as Google Play Services or AdMob for functionality and advertising. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

## Children's Information
This app is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time.

## Contact
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer.
